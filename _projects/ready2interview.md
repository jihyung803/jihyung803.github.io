Ready2Interview was a HackTX project for interview practice and real-time feedback. The system combined user-document RAG, internet-search RAG, ASR, and LLM feedback loops.

**Focus**

- Retrieval over resumes, CVs, and other PDFs.
- Real-time speech-to-text interaction.
- LLM feedback for interview preparation.
