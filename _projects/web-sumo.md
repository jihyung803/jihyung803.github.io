Web-SUMO connects regional traffic data with simulation workflows for urban mobility analysis. The project explores how digital twins can support road modification analysis and optimal traffic-system design.

**Focus**

- Traffic data collection and preprocessing.
- SUMO-based simulation workflows.
- Reinforcement-learning ideas for transportation planning.
