SafeMate is a crisis-preparedness chatbot and web system developed through Urban Information Lab work with the City of Austin. The system helps users prepare for and respond to emergency situations through a practical LLM-assisted workflow.

**Focus**

- Chatbot pipeline design.
- Web interface development.
- Public-interest AI for crisis information access.
