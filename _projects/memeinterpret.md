MemeInterpret builds on the Facebook Hateful Memes dataset with captions, surface messages, and relevant background knowledge. The goal is to support more complete evaluation of multimodal meme understanding.

**Focus**

- Meme caption and interpretation annotation.
- Background-knowledge collection.
- Multimodal language understanding evaluation.
