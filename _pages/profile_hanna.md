### Hanna Cho

Hanna is a service designer at the City of Austin's Innovation Office and co-leads the Civic Agents Observatory. She bridges real constraints from frontline staff into our research prototypes.

- Designs community research plans and policy workshops
- Defines multilingual response guidelines and safety scenarios
- Documents governance processes for sustained public deployments

Together we're testing models with Austin's 3-1-1 team along with Parks and Transportation departments.
