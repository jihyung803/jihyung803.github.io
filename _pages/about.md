I am a computer science researcher at The University of Texas at Austin and a graduate research assistant in the Urban Information Lab. My work focuses on LLMs, multimodal understanding, and practical AI systems for urban and public-interest settings.

I received my B.S. in Computer Science from The University of Texas at Dallas, where I worked with the Human Language Technology Research Institute on meme understanding. I have also worked on LLM data generation and validation as an LLM trainer at Turing.
