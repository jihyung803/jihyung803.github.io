Hello. I am a computer science researcher who recently completed an M.S. in Computer Science at The University of Texas at Austin, where I worked as a graduate research assistant in the Urban Information Lab.

My work focuses on language models, multimodal understanding, and practical AI systems for public-interest settings. I am especially interested in pragmatic reasoning, self-reinforcing training, safety monitoring, digital twins, and multimodal datasets.

Before UT Austin, I received my B.S. in Computer Science from The University of Texas at Dallas, where I worked with the Human Language Technology Research Institute on meme understanding. I have also worked on LLM data generation and validation as an LLM trainer at Turing.
